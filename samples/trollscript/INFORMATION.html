This file gives information about the samples provide in this directory. Just to note, none of these were created by me unless otherwise stated.

  • hello_world.troll: prints "Hello World!" to the console.
  • fibbonacci.troll: prints the first 16 numbers of the fibbonacci sequence
  • troll_art.troll: prints out pixel-art of a "troll face"". If it looks strange to you, try viewing it from farther away.
