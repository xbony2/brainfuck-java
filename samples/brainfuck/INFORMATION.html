This file gives information about the samples provide in this directory. Just to note, none of these were created by me unless otherwise stated.

  • hello_world.bf: prints "Hello World!" to the console.
  • cat.bf: asks for input, and then returns what you had inputed; an echo program.
  • self_interpreter.bf: this is a self-interpreter made by Daniel B. Cristofani. More information here. I recommend not looking over if you are looking for something basic to start with, it may be a little bit confusing.
